About Us!

If you have ever wanted to get back in shape. Be a better you. A STRONGER YOU. Then you have come to the right place! Here at this this website we have created a community that will not only gauge you in what you already know about your own fitness and health but we can then teach you what else you may need to know and give you the tools you need to be succesful in your endeavors!

Once you think you have got a good idea of where your at and what you need to do to get to where you want to be, dont forget to track your progress by creating your own blog and watch as you change and grow into the man/woman you always knew you could be. Don't forget to help everyone else on this journey with you by tracking their blogs and giving helpful motivational tips as they good on their ways too!

Where to begin

Begin with signing up to create your very own account, and from there go on over to our learn section and start learning about various exercises and stretches. After that, it is time to make your very first post and come up with a plan for where you want to be and how you are going to get there! Don't forget to upload progress pics along the way. Remember, here you are a part of a community and we are going to keep track of you as much as you keep track of yourself!
